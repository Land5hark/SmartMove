# SmartMove Design System & Product Direction

Version 1.0

# Product Vision

SmartMove is not just a moving app.

SmartMove is an AI-powered moving memory system.

The product helps users inventory, organize, locate, and retrieve everything they own while reducing the stress and cognitive overload associated with moving.

**The emotional positioning is:**

"Moving doesn't have to feel chaotic."

**The functional positioning is:**

"AI remembers what's in every box so you don't have to."

# Core Screens

## 1\. Welcome Screen

Purpose:

Create excitement.

Elements:

\- Sunset background  
\- Animated logo  
\- SmartMove wordmark  
\- Short value statement

Copy:

Remember every box.

Find anything instantly.

\---

## 2\. Home Dashboard

Show:

\- Upcoming move date  
\- Packing progress  
\- Boxes packed  
\- AI insights  
\- Room completion status

Large visual progress indicators.

Not spreadsheets.

Not lists.

\---

## 3\. Boxes Screen

Display boxes as visual cards.

Each card includes:

\- Photo  
\- Room assignment  
\- Item count  
\- Box ID  
\- Quick scan button

Cards should feel collectible.

Almost like a media library.

\---

## 4\. Box Detail Screen

Current functionality stays.

Visual improvements:

\- Larger photos  
\- Better tag chips  
\- Cleaner hierarchy  
\- AI-generated inventory summary

Example:

Contains altar items, ritual tools, and decorative objects.

Likely belongs in Master Bedroom.

\---

## 5\. Scan Screen

Hero feature.

Should feel magical.

Flow:

Take photo

↓

AI analyzes contents

↓

Items detected

↓

Suggested room

↓

Generate QR

↓

Save box

Must be visually impressive.

This is the app's "wow moment."

\---

## 6\. Move Timeline

New screen.

Visual timeline:

30 days before move

14 days before move

7 days before move

Moving day

Unpacking phase

AI automatically recommends tasks.

\---

## 7\. Smart Search

Examples:

"Where is my passport?"

"Find tarot deck"

"Show altar items"

Results instantly return:

Room

Box

Photo

Tags

\---

# Motion Design

Everything should feel alive.

Use:

\- Soft fades  
\- Neon glow transitions  
\- Floating gradients  
\- Smooth loading animations

Avoid:

\- Bounce effects  
\- Gimmicks  
\- Excessive motion

\---

# Empty States

Never show blank screens.

Example:

No Boxes Yet

Display:

Neon sunset grid

Floating box icon

Message:

Your next chapter starts with the first box.

\---

# Accessibility

Large tap targets.

High contrast.

Readable fonts.

Simple hierarchy.

Especially important for:

\- ADHD users  
\- Neurodivergent users  
\- Stressed users during a move

\---

# Product Goal

When someone opens SmartMove they should feel:

"This isn't a moving app."

"This feels like having an AI assistant helping me organize my entire life.”
